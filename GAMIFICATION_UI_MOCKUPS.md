## 9. Animation Specs

### Level Up Animation
- **Duration**: 2 seconds
- **Easing**: `cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94)`
- **Effects**: Scale, fade, particle burst
- **Sound**: Optional subtle chime

### XP Gain Notification
- **Duration**: 1.5 seconds
- **Motion**: Slide up, fade out
- **Easing**: Ease out

### Badge Unlock
- **Duration**: 1 second
- **Effects**: Glow, scale, shine
- **Sound**: Optional unlock sound

### Progress Bar Fill
- **Duration**: 0.5 seconds
- **Easing**: Ease out
- **Gradient**: Animated shimmer (optional)
